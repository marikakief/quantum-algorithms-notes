## What it does
Sharpens [[Order-Condition Cancellation in Product Formulas|product-formula error]] analysis by treating all-distinct-index terms separately from repeated-index terms.

## The trick
Exploit permutation symmetry to cancel/control nondegenerate terms, then bound degenerate leftovers combinatorially.

## When to reach for it
- High-quality analytic error bounds for randomized or symmetrized formulas

## Complexity
Improves $L$-scaling in theoretical error bounds.

## Caveat
Technical counting arguments can become intricate at high order.
