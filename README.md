# quantum-algorithms-notes
A personal Obsidian knowledge base for quantum algorithms, complexity, and related theory. Started in early 2026 as a way to get back into the literature after a research hiatus.

What this is

Structured notes on quantum algorithms papers, built around a network of paper summaries and reusable "trick cards" — atomic technique notes that can be linked across papers. The goal is a graph of connected ideas rather than a flat list of summaries.

What this isn't

• Complete. Papers were added based on whatever I was reading or thinking about at the time, not systematically. Large and important areas are missing. If your paper isn't here, it's not a comment on its quality.
• Error-free. Notes were generated with AI assistance (Claude/Sergey) and reviewed by me, but I can't guarantee every complexity bound, proof sketch, or cross-reference is correct. If you spot an error, open an issue or email me.
• A reference. Read the actual papers. These notes are for navigation and connection-building, not as a substitute for the source material.

Structure

Quantum Foundations/
├── Paper Summaries/     — one note per paper
├── Quantum Tricks/      — atomic reusable technique cards
└── Hamiltonian Simulation — Comparison Tables.md

AI disclosure

Notes were written with significant AI assistance. I reviewed and edited everything, but the drafts came from Claude. I've done my best to catch errors, but the usual caveats apply — especially on complexity bounds and proof details.
