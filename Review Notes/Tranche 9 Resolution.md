# Tranche 9 Resolution

Scope: first-quantized chemistry, arbitrary-basis qubitization, THC, QROM/QROAM, and 2024 pseudopotential feedback from the second review set onward.

## Paper Notes

- `Exponentially More Precise Quantum Simulation of Fermions in the CI Representation`: implemented the qubit-count correction (`eta log N` versus `N`), clarified that the title's exponential claim is about precision scaling, fixed the `eta^2 << N` gate-comparison condition, corrected the `N=100`, `eta=10` arithmetic, separated the NJP second-quantized Taylor paper from the CI paper, and scoped the orbital-regularity assumptions.
- `Encoding Electronic Spectra in Quantum Circuits with Linear T Complexity`: scoped the plane-wave-dual `lambda=O(N^2)` claim, fixed garbage-register language for PREPARE/reflection/PREPARE-dagger, corrected the Hubbard scaling to `O(N lambda / epsilon)`, separated system/ancilla/physical qubit counts, and labeled QROM counts as 2018 T-gate accounting.
- `Low Rank Representations`: clarified that the chemically accurate result is a factorization/truncation benchmark rather than a Trotter-error guarantee, fixed the fermionic-swap-network comparison, and added the bridge to later double-factorized qubitization.
- `Qubitization of Arbitrary Basis Quantum Chemistry`: restored `/Delta E` in phase-estimation costs, scoped `L=O(N)` as empirical, changed the 700x claim to surface-code spacetime rather than Toffoli count, removed the invalid molecular-orbital FeMoCo comparison for the plane-wave-dual algorithm, and marked sparse thresholding as an empirical Hamiltonian approximation.
- `Quantum Simulation of Chemistry with Sublinear Scaling in Basis Size`: corrected the CI comparison ratio to `N^(8/3)/eta^(2/3)`, fixed the Low-Wiebe ratio discussion, replaced the overly simple kinetic-dominance explanation with the actual `N >> eta^6` condition, fixed the qubitization alternative crossover at `N = eta^4`, and scoped the Born-Oppenheimer Hamiltonian.
- `Fault-Tolerant Quantum Simulations of Chemistry in First Quantization`: scoped the resource-estimate claims to the studied first-quantized circuits, replaced the simplified failed-PREP normalization with the theorem-level caveat, defined the QROM/nuclear-position cost more carefully, qualified qubitized-Dyson savings, and replaced the non-Born-Oppenheimer claim with an extension caveat.
- `Even More Efficient Quantum Computations of Chemistry Through Tensor Hypercontraction`: corrected the non-orthogonal diagonal-Coulomb language, removed the universal `lambda` ordering, fixed the per-step scaling explanation to the theorem-level `~O(N)` statement, and softened the optimality and 2025 spectral-amplification update.
- `Quantum Simulation of Realistic Materials in First Quantization Using Non-Local Pseudopotentials`: scoped "first complete block encoding" to the first-quantized plane-wave/GTH setting, clarified the address-register sense of the arithmetic-over-QROM improvement, updated QPE Toffoli scale to `10^14`-`10^15`, exposed the `lambda_nonloc` tradeoff in the construction, and fixed the angular-momentum-projector comparison row.

## Trick Cards

- `QROM`, `QROAM`, `Measurement-Based QROM Uncomputation`, `Coherent Alias Sampling`, and `Unary Iteration`: separated 2018 QROM/unary T-gate accounting from later QROAM Toffoli accounting, distinguished coherent inverse uncomputation from measurement-based cleanup, and removed the claim that garbage can simply be traced out or reset.
- `Single Factorization`, `Sparse State Preparation`, and `Double Factorization`: restored phase-estimation precision factors, scoped rank/sparsity assumptions as empirical, marked thresholding as a Hamiltonian approximation, and added compatibility caveats for dirty ancillae and symmetry-controlled swaps.
- `THC Non-Orthogonal Diagonal Coulomb Representation` and `QROM-Loaded Givens Rotation Networks`: clarified that THC uses term-dependent unitary rotations rather than a global orthonormal diagonal basis, scoped `Gamma=O(N^2)` to empirical THC rank, and separated angle loading from rotation synthesis cost.
- `Kinetic Energy as Bit-Product LCU`, `Failed PREP Fallback`, `Incremental Kinetic Energy Register`, `Interaction Picture Simulation`, and `Qubitized Dyson Series`: scoped the kinetic trick to qubitized block encoding, marked failed-PREP formulas as idealized intuition unless theorem corrections are included, qualified the Dyson constant-factor savings, and fixed the Low-Wiebe source attribution.
- `QROM Interpolation`, `Gramian-Based Momentum Norm`, `Arithmetic vs QROM`, and `Shared Exponential Evaluation`: clarified interpolation table-size versus total function-evaluation cost, scoped crystal-class simplifications to examples, stated the arithmetic/QROM tradeoff criterion including `lambda` inflation, and narrowed the shared-exponential trick to the GTH/HGH-style parameterization.

No supervisor suggestion in this tranche required rejecting the feedback outright; the main resolutions were to implement the correction directly or to narrow the statement to the regime actually supported by the cited paper.
