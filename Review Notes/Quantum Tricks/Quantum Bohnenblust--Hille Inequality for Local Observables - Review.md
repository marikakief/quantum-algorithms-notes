# Review: Quantum Bohnenblust--Hille Inequality for Local Observables

Source note: [[Quantum Bohnenblust--Hille Inequality for Local Observables]]

## Primary Sources Checked

- Huang, Chen, and Preskill, arXiv:2210.14894 / PRX Quantum 4, 040337 (2023).

## Verdict

Minor issues. This is a good compact card. It needs constants/qualification around locality and bounded degree.

## Line-Anchored Comments

- Lines 18-24: the inequality direction is right as a mnemonic: coefficient norm is controlled by operator norm up to constants depending on locality.
- Line 24: "effective l1 control" should be tied to bounded-degree observables. It is not a blanket statement for all `k`-local Hamiltonians with many overlapping terms.
- Lines 26-32: the link to thresholded learning is good.
- Lines 38-40: good caveat. Keep it near the theorem statement too.

## Missing or Suggested Additions

- Add the constant dependence on `k` or at least say it can grow with locality.
