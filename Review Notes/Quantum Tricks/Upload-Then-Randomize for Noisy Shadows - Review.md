# Review: Upload-Then-Randomize for Noisy Shadows

Source note: [[Upload-Then-Randomize for Noisy Shadows]]

## Primary Sources Checked

- Kannan, Putterman, and Cotler, arXiv:2605.02057, checked current arXiv v1.

## Verdict

Minor issues. The card is clear and useful. The only needed edits are around metric labels and support assumptions.

## Line-Anchored Comments

- Lines 12-20: the raw noisy shallow-shadow weight is well motivated.
- Lines 22-41: the ordering contrast is exactly the right conceptual point.
- Lines 51-66: specify that the stated sample-complexity and ratio apply to the paper's shallow-shadow setup and small-noise parameter regime, not arbitrary classical-shadow protocols.
- Line 70: good caveat about the surviving input-noise penalty.

## Missing or Suggested Additions

- Add whether `k` is Pauli support size, contiguous support size, or an arbitrary locality parameter in each displayed bound.
