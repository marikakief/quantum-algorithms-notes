# Review: Holevo Information Bottleneck for Learning Lower Bounds

Source note: [[Holevo Information Bottleneck for Learning Lower Bounds]]

## Primary Sources Checked

- Huang, Kueng, and Preskill, arXiv:2101.02464 / Physical Review Letters 126, 190505 (2021).

## Verdict

Minor issues. Accurate and concise. It should distinguish lower-bound logic from the matching restricted-classical learner construction.

## Line-Anchored Comments

- Lines 12-24: the packing-to-communication explanation is good.
- Lines 30-34: the card says this machinery underlies the average-case result. Add that the paper also constructs a restricted classical learner; the Holevo argument is part of bounding possible quantum advantage, not the entire proof of an efficient classical algorithm.
- Lines 36-38: good caveat. Keep this because it is the most common overreading of information-theoretic lower bounds.
