# Review: Operator Differential Method for Commutator Product Formulas

Source note: [[Operator Differential Method for Commutator Product Formulas]]

## Primary Sources Checked

- Chen et al., arXiv:2111.12177: https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.12177

## Verdict

Sound with minor caveats.

## Line-Anchored Comments

- Lines 10-14: the formula is too compressed to be useful for most readers. It is probably correct as a schematic, but the "appropriate operator" placeholder makes it hard to verify or reuse.
- Line 14: the reduction to `l,m,q,r,s` is the actionable part and should be foregrounded.
- Lines 24-25: good caveat. Solving the polynomial order conditions, not applying the method, is the bottleneck.

## Missing or Suggested Additions

- Add the explicit six-gate `S_3(x)` coefficient solution from the paper note as the worked example.
