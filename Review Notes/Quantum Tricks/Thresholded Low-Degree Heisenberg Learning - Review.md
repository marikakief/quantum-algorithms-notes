# Review: Thresholded Low-Degree Heisenberg Learning

Source note: [[Thresholded Low-Degree Heisenberg Learning]]

## Primary Sources Checked

- Huang, Chen, and Preskill, arXiv:2210.14894 / PRX Quantum 4, 040337 (2023).

## Verdict

Minor issues. The card is accurate and well scoped.

## Line-Anchored Comments

- Lines 12-28: the Heisenberg-picture truncation and thresholding story is clear.
- Line 30: "input-state distribution kills high-degree terms" should name the paper's condition: locally flat or similarly structured product/distribution assumptions.
- Lines 38-44: good complexity and caveat. Add that quasi-polynomial precision dependence is part of the cost.

## Missing or Suggested Additions

- Add a direct link to the quantum Bohnenblust-Hille card in the main text, not only related notes, because that inequality is what makes thresholding safe.
